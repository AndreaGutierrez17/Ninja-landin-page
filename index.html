Shadow’s Guardian

Fast, fluid, and fierce. A new action 2D platformer is forging its way to mobile.

Get the app (soon) Watch gameplay

Get ready to step into the shadows with the deadliest guardian of light. In this electrifying 2D action-platformer, a fierce kunoichi warrior must unleash the ancient techniques of her clan to fight back the relentless flood of evil. Master a fluid combat system, unleash dazzling combos, shuriken storms, and silent takedowns against hordes of cunning enemies. Each level leads to an epic showdown with a unique monstrous boss that will push your skill and strategy to the limit. The balance between good and evil is in your hands. Sharpen your blades and stay determined—the fight for justice is coming soon to mobile app stores.
